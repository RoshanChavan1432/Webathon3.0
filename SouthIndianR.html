Dhosa

Ingrideants

  1. 500 gm Basmati Biryani Rice
  2. 200 gm Onion
  3. 1 TableSpoon ginger Garlic pest
  4. 100 gm Carrot
  5. 100 gm Tomato
  6. 1 TableSpoon Red chilli Powder
  7. 0.5 TableSpoon termeric Powder
  8. 100 gm soyabean chunks
  9. 100 gm flower
  10. 50 gm whole spices
  11. Salt (To test)
  12. 3.5 cup Water
  13. 100 gm Potato
  14. 100 gm Paneer
  15. 50 gm ghee
  1. Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.
  2. Heat the ghee in a pan, add the cinnamon, cloves, green cardamom,
    star anise, peppercorns and black cardamom. Then, add the onions
    and sauté till they turn golden-brown.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and stir-fry till the raw smell is gone.
    Add the red chilli powder, turmeric powder and
    coriander powder and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes and cook till they are soft.
    Add all the vegetables and mix well.
  5. Reduce flame to the lowest and cook covered till
    both the rice and vegetables are done.
  6. Serve hot with raita.

  2. 1/2 cup Green Peas
  3. 3/4 cup chopped Cauliflower
  4. 1/2 cup chopped Carrot
  5. 1 large Onion, chopped
  6. 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste
  7. 2 medium Tomatoes, chopped
  8. 1/2 cup chopped Capsicum s
  9. 1½ teaspoons Red Chilli Powder
  10. 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  11. 1 teaspoon Cumin-Coriander Powder, optional
  12. 1 teaspoon Readymade Pav Bhaji Masala Powder/li>
  13. 1 teaspoon Lemon Juice
  14. Salt to taste
  15. 2 tablespoons Oil + 2 tablespoons Butter
  16. Butter for serving
  17. 2 tablespoons finely chopped Coriander Leaves
  18. 8 Pav Buns, for serving
  1. Take all the vegetables listed in the ingredients.
  2. Transfer chopped potato, cauliflower, carrot and green peas
    into a 2-3 liter capacity pressure cooker. Add 1.5 cup water and br salt to taste. and cook over medium flame for 2-whistles.
  3. Mash the boiled vegetables gently with potato masher or using
    the backside of a large spoon until little chunky texture.
  4. Heat 2-tablespoons oil and 2-tablespoons butter together in a
    pan over medium flame. Add chopped onion and ginger-garlic paste.
    Sauté until onion turns translucent.
  5. Add chopped capsicum, chopped tomato and salt.
    Sauté until tomatoes and capsicum turn soft.
  6. Add 1½ teaspoons red chilli powder, 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder,
    1-teaspoon cumin-coriander powder and 1-teaspoon readymade

  7. Stir and cook for a minute.
    Add 3/4 cup water, mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add boiled and mashed vegetables and 1-teaspoon lemon juice.
    Mix well and cook for 4-5 minutes. Taste for the salt at this stage
    and add more if required. Bhaji is ready for serving.
  9. Cut the pav buns horizontally into halves. Heat tava over
    medium flame Transfer prepared bhaji to a serving bowl
    Serve hot with butter roasted pav, sliced onion and lemon wedges.

Fish Curry

Ingrideants

  1. 2 big onion
  2. 6 Eggs
  3. 1 tablespoon Oil
  4. 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste
  5. ½ cup Finely chopped Tomatoes
  6. 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder
  7. 1 teaspoon Cumin Powder
  8. ½ teaspoon Red Chilli powder
  9. ½ teaspoon Turmeric powder
  10. ½ teaspoon Garma Masala
  11. 1 tablespoon Kasuri Methi
  12. 1.5 cup Water
  13. salt to Taste
  1. Step
    Boil Egge for 5 to 8 min on medium flame after that peel it.
  2. Toss peeled boiled eggs in 1/4 each of salt, turmeric, and chili powder.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. When the oil is hot, add eggs to the pan. Toss in
    medium-hot until you see small brown blisters. Remove and keep aside.
  4. To the same pan add 1 tablespoon of oil and butter. When hot add
    dry red chili and saute for 30 seconds until smokey.
  5. Add chopped onion, saute for 3-4 minutes. The onion will soften and start to brown.
  6. Add ginger-garlic paste. Saute for a minute, stirring in between.
  7. When there is no raw smell of ginger garlic, lower heat. Add cumin
    powder, coriander powder, turmeric, red chilli powder,
    Kashmiri red chilli powder.
  8. Add chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomato is soft and mushy.
  9. Add chopped green chilli and crushed Kasuri methi. Mix well.
  10. When you see oil separating from the mixture, it is time to add water.
  11. Season with salt, add garam masala and fried eggs to the pan.
  12. Mix everything and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  13. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Ingrideants

  1. 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  2. 1 small yellow onion, chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 tablespoon curry powder
  5. 1 14.5 oz. can diced fire-roasted tomatoes, drained
  6. ⅓ cup low-sodium chicken broth
  7. ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  8. ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  9. ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper, optional
  10. 1 ½ cups cooked chicken, chopped
  11. ¾ cup plain Greek yogurt
  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and sauté for 4-5 minutes, until softened.
    Add garlic and sauté another 30 seconds.
  3. Stir in curry powder, diced tomatoes, and chicken broth.
    Season with salt, pepper and cayenne, if using.
  4. Bring to a low simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, until slightly thickened.
  5. Stir in chicken until warmed through, 1-2 more minutes.
  6. Turn off the heat and stir in the Greek yogurt until just combined.
  7. Serve hot with fresh chopped parsley or cilantro and chopped peanuts.
